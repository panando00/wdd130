White Water Rafting

White Water Rafting's mission is to provide an unforgettable experience on the water, combining adventure, nature, and fun for people of all ages and skill levels. Our proffesional guides are dedicated to ensuring your safety enjoyment, while our state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly practices guarantee a seamless and responsible journey through nature.

Our History

Our company was founded in the early 2000s by a group of passionate rafters who wanted to share their love of whitewater rafting with the world. Our rafting company started as a small, local operation, providing guided tours on the wild and scenic rivers in our region. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading rafting companies in the country, offering a wide range of trips for all skill levels and ages.

With a team of experienced guides, we are proud to offer unfforgetable rafting experiences on some of the most breathtaking rivers in the world, from raging rapids of Panashe to the serene, scenic waterfalls of the Zvidzai Mountains. We believe that rafting is more than just a thrill-seeking activity, it is a way to connect with nature, experience breathtaking landscapes, and create lasting memories
